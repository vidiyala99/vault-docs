NORTHWIND MUTUAL INSURANCE COMPANY
COMMERCIAL PROPERTY POLICY

Policy Number: NW-CP-2026-114829
Named Insured: Cascade Cold Storage LLC
Mailing Address: 4410 Harbor Industrial Way, Tacoma, WA 98421
Policy Period: March 1, 2026 to March 1, 2027, 12:01 a.m. Standard Time
Annual Premium: $84,300

SECTION I - COVERED PROPERTY

Coverage applies to the building located at 4410 Harbor Industrial Way,
Tacoma, WA 98421, including permanently installed refrigeration machinery
and equipment. The building limit of insurance is $6,500,000. Business
personal property is covered up to a limit of $1,200,000.

Newly acquired buildings are covered automatically for up to 90 days,
subject to a maximum limit of $500,000 per location.

SECTION II - DEDUCTIBLES

The property deductible is $25,000 per occurrence. A separate wind and
hail deductible of 2% of the building limit applies to loss caused by
windstorm or hail. The wind and hail deductible is never less than
$50,000 for any one occurrence.

SECTION III - BUSINESS INCOME

Business income coverage, including extra expense, is provided up to
$2,000,000 with a waiting period of 72 hours. The period of restoration
is limited to 12 consecutive months following the date of direct
physical loss.

SECTION IV - EXCLUSIONS

This policy does not cover loss caused by flood, including surface
water, waves, tidal water, or water that backs up through sewers or
drains. Loss caused by earth movement, including earthquake and
landslide, is excluded. Loss caused by spoilage due to power failure
originating off the described premises is excluded unless the
Off-Premises Power endorsement is attached.

Wear and tear, rust, corrosion, and gradual deterioration are not
covered. Theft by any employee of the named insured is excluded and
must be insured under a separate crime policy.

SECTION V - CONDITIONS

The named insured must report any vacancy exceeding 60 consecutive days.
If the building has been vacant for more than 60 consecutive days before
a loss, vandalism and water damage losses are not covered, and all other
covered losses are reduced by 15%.

In the event of loss, the named insured must give prompt notice to
Northwind Mutual and submit a signed, sworn proof of loss within 90 days
of the company's request. Coinsurance of 90% applies to the building
limit. Appraisal may be demanded by either party when the parties
disagree on the value of the property or the amount of the loss.

Cancellation by the company requires 60 days advance written notice,
or 10 days for non-payment of premium.
